About Lifeline

Lifeline Pregnancy Resource Center is dedicated to empowering individuals to
make healthy life choices. Lifeline is a non-profit organization based in
Kirksville, Missouri, serving the northeast Missouri region.

We are located in downtown Kirksville at 306 W. Washington Street.  Our
office is directly across from the Traveler's Hotel.  If you need assistance or
directions, please call us at 660-665-5688.

Since we cover such a large area, there may be a closer center if you live
outside of the Kirksville area.
