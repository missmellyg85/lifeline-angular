Ever wish fatherhood came with an instruction manual? Do you wonder how other dads deal with the responsibilities and challenges of being a dad? Would you like a safe place to ask questions and learn about caring for your baby without being embarrassed or feeling like you’re being judged? Now you can get the answers to all your questions and more in a safe, “dads only” environment. DadLINE is here for you!

Lifeline Pregnancy Help Clinic is pleased to announce this new program! DadLINE will teach new fathers the basics of fatherhood as well as address challenges or concerns that a new father may face. Group meets at Lifeline weekly, every Thursday at 5:15 pm. If you would like to sign up for this free program, please call Lifeline at 665-5688 or email us here!
